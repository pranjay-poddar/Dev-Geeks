About Project

We all are staying in the society who follows democracy. And in democracy, It is the responsibility of the government that the public places and other services that are managed by the government are well maintained and also up to date because If these services are not maintained then problems occur for the common people. This CityTrack website keeps track of the form that is filled out by the common people. Also, when the problem is occurring, common people have to go to different government bodies’ offices and make complaints about it and It is very difficult to handle all these things. This CityTrack also helps you to figure out all these things and also keeps a record of all these things. In general, If common people want to make a complaint to one government body or want to make a complaint to more than one government body, they just have to fill out only one form and this form will circulate to all government bodies. Also, the common people keep a record of these filled complaints and also see the information about the government employee and the work that is done by them. Also, the common people discuss something with all India’s people in the discussion section. We build this website for the common people and also for the government bodies from which they can see the pending complaint and completed works.
