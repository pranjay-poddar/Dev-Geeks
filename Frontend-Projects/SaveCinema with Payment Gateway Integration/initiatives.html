Most Hyped Upcoming Tamil Film

Thalapathy Vijay's Varisu

Varisu is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language drama film directed by Vamshi Paidipally who wrote the film with Hari and Ahishor Solomon. Produced by Dil Raju and Sirish under Sri Venkateswara Creations, the film stars Vijay and Rashmika Mandanna. Principal photography began in April 2022 in Chennai. The film is scheduled to be released in theatres in January 2023 on the occasion of Pongal.

Most Hyped Upcoming Hindi Film

Sharukh Khan's Jawan

Jawan is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language action thriller film written and directed by Atlee. It stars Shah Rukh Khan in a dual role alongside Vijay Sethupathi, Nayanthara, Priyamani, Sanya Malhotra and Deepika Padukone. The film's music is composed by Anirudh Ravichander. Principal photography of the film commenced in September 2021 with filming taking place in Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad and Chennai. It is scheduled for theatrical release on 2 June 2023.

Most Hyped Upcoming Telugu Film

Prabhas's Saalar

Salaar is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action thriller film written and directed by Prashanth Neel, and produced by Vijay Kiragandur under Hombale Films. It stars Prabhas as the titular character alongside Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan and Jagapathi Babu.Salaar was originally scheduled for a theatrical release on 14 April 2022 but was delayed due to production delays owing to the COVID-19 pandemic. The film is scheduled to be theatrically released on 28 September 2023.
