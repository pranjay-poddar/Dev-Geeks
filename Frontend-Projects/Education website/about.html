About

We are a group of four developers working to solve challenges that students face on a regular basis. When we want to learn a skill or a particular mot, we often search on Google. If we want to study through sites, we search on Google, if we want to study through videos, we search on YouTube, and if we want to do a course related to that skill, we search on Google again.

To make this procedure as simple as possible, we created a website where you can receive all of your content in one spot. If a student wants to learn python, he or she will be able to find all types of content, including written content on websites, videos on YouTube, and courses on the subject.
