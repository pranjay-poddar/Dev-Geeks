IIT Kanpur GATE Exam Hall Ticket 2023

Aspirants applied for GATE 2023 can download their GATE Exam Hall Ticket 2023 via, gate.iitk.ac.in. aspirants will be able to download their GATE Admit Card 2023 from 9 January 2023 till the date of commencement of exam i.e. 4 February 2023. For this, aspirants have to fill their details like registration number, date of birth, etc. and submit. GATE exam is proposed to be organized by the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur on 4, 5, 11 and 12 February 2023.

GATE Exam Pattern

According to the notification released for GATE 2023, the exam will be organized in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode. The duration of the exam will be 3 hours and will be conducted for a total of 29 subjects/papers. The question paper will consist of General Aptitude and 10+55=65) questions from the subject/paper chosen by the aspirant. These questions will be Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), Multiple Select Questions (MSQs) and Numerical Answer Type (NAT) in nature. Maximum marks are fixed for General Aptitude 15 and for subject 85 marks so aspirants exam date is one month from today get your Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering Admit Card 2023 on 9 January 2023.
