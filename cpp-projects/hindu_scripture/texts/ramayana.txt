Ramayana Excerpt:
"Rama, the best among men, the abode of virtue, the enhancer of Kausalya's delight, was the first son to the King Dasaratha. Rama was endowed with great glory, ever rejoicing the hearts of all people. In bowmanship, none was equal to him, and in the use of the sword, he was superior to all.

The illustrious Rama, the epitome of righteousness, grew up in the royal palace of Ayodhya, loved and admired by his subjects and family alike. His divine qualities and charming personality endeared him to all who came across him. He possessed unmatched valor, and his skill with the bow was so exceptional that it earned him the title of the greatest archer in the world.
