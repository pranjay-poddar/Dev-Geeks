Garg Samhita Excerpt:
"The son of Garga said: O greatest sage, best of the speakers of eloquent prayers, by what process does the soul travel through the various species of life, and what is its ultimate destination?"

In this excerpt, the son of Garga, one of the characters in the Garg Samhita, is inquiring about the journey of the soul
through different life forms and seeking to understand the ultimate destination of the soul. The Garg Samhita is a sacred text in Hinduism that contains various stories, teachings,
and dialogues on spiritual subjects, including the nature of the soul and the paths to liberation.
